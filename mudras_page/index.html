Mudras

Mudras are symbolic hand gestures that are important in yoga, meditation, and Indian classical dance because they can:
1. Balance the body
2.Improve mental activity
3.Relieve stress

Pataka

A hand gesture symbolizing a flag or stop.

Tripataka

A hand gesture symbolizing a crown or tree.

Simhamukha

A hand gesture symbolizing representing division, balance, or unity in duality.

Kartarimukha

A hand gesture symbolizing scissors or separation.

Arala

A hand gesture symbolizing a bent hand or wind.

Shukatunda

A hand gesture symbolizing a parrot's beak.
